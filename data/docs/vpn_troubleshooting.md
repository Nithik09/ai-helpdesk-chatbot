# VPN Troubleshooting Guide

**Audience:** Employees and IT agents

## Quick Checks
1. Confirm internet connectivity.
2. Verify VPN client version is current.
3. Ensure your account is not locked.

## Steps
- Disconnect and reconnect the VPN client.
- If error 809/720 appears, restart your device.
- If multi-factor fails, contact IT to reset MFA.

## Escalation
If VPN cannot connect after the above steps, open a ticket with:
- Error code
- Device OS
- Time of attempt
